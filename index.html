Welcome to Sparkzlab

Idea

Summer 2016. During the year, we had spent a lot of our free time basking in the huge amount of information the internet has made available for us. For the past 3 years we had become avid business and technology blog readers, getting eager over those top 10 articles on “skills you should have” or “programming languages you should learn”. Summer 2015 we drew the line. Less planning and more applying! We took advantage of our student status in order to get free trials on online courses and completely changed our YouTube recommendation section into the most educational (and geeky) one you will ever see.

And so it happened that we returned to our hometown in Spain for a holiday, and immediately we felt what was missing: life-skills education. Schools in Spain base their education system on memorising and regurgitating information that will not necessarily be useful for the student’s future. And while schools in northern Europe dedicate time and energy to preparing students for an entrepreneurial or professional life, this was not what we received in Spain…

It so happens that boot-camps are starting to pick up popularity all over Spain. But there is an issue: they are expensive and limited.

For us, the solution was clear: to create our own program to give back what we wish we had been told when we were finishing our studies. To show students what free resources there are and to show them how to motivate themselves to follow their passion.

Our strengths

We are a pretty eager bunch, and we are very proud of our team and of what we do. Sparkzlab gives young people the opportunity to overcome barriers. Our purpose is to help people archieve their life goals by providing the right knowledge and guidance. We provide life long advice, knowledge and a supoortive community of professionals through our exclusive Sparkzlab Alumni platform.


Innovation

Our international experience allows us to create innovative and unique teaching methods.

Diversity

The diverse background of Sprakzlab members fosters creative problem solving.

Partners

Our partners are a group of successful professionals, innovative entrepreneurs and award winning professors from top Universities.

Our topics

After many hours and countless coffees, we managed to hand-pick the topics we found to be the most important for the budding entrepreneur or professional. In a world as technological and scientifically advanced that we live in today it may be hard to find your place. But after finishing our program we guarantee our students will have a much better understanding of how to pursue their ambition and maybe even change the world!
